General Info

The following is a description of the columns in the training table below.

Status
The status of the training. Possible values are :
- Up To Date
- Completed
- Past Due
Title
The title of the training
Due Date
The date on which the training must be complete.
Date Completed
The actual date the training was completed.
Actions
A list of the actions for the particular training
- Run the Training
- Mark this training as Completed
